## Design Context

### Users
- Event hosts (weddings, conferences, parties, meetups) who need a reliable live photo wall with minimal setup overhead.
- Guests joining from mobile in high-distraction environments who need the fastest path from invite to upload.
- Primary job to be done: hosts launch and moderate a live wall quickly; guests join and contribute photos without app install friction.

### Brand Personality
- Memorable, trustworthy, and energetic.
- The experience should feel immediate and celebratory while still operationally dependable for live events.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark, cinematic stage-like atmosphere with warm accent highlights.
- Avoid generic SaaS card-grid repetition and decorative-only interactions that compete with conversion actions.
- Mobile-first conversion clarity: thumb-friendly actions and obvious host/guest path separation.

### Design Principles
1. Clarify user path in first 5 seconds: split host and guest intent explicitly.
2. Prioritize trust before flourish: reliability cues and concrete value signals near primary CTAs.
3. Keep decorative motion subordinate to conversion and readability.
4. Ensure touch ergonomics and reduced-motion support as first-class polish requirements.
5. Preserve bilingual parity between English and Chinese copy and hierarchy.

### Quality Bar
- Flagship-level landing page polish: conversion clarity, consistency, and interaction detail should be production-ready.
